# Construct Card — Agreeableness

## Purpose
Test whether a broader Big Five slice gives a cleaner or more interpretable parent construct around sycophancy-like behavior.

## In Scope
- warm, cooperative, prosocial stance
- cold or antagonistic stance as the opposing pole

## Out of Scope
- blind validation of user beliefs
- direct deception or manipulativeness
- pure politeness without broader social stance

## Why This Family Matters
- Paper-backed personality framing.
- Could contextualize sycophancy as a narrower special case.

## Main Confounds
- trait entanglement with helpfulness and sycophancy
- broader open-ended personality framing can weaken extraction sharpness

## Screening Guidance
- Compare directly against sycophancy rather than treating as fully independent by default.
