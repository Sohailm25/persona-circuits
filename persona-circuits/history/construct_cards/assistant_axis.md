# Construct Card — Assistant Axis

## Purpose
Screen for a broad assistant-likeness / persona-drift axis that is closer to persona-space structure than a narrow single trait.

## In Scope
- assistant-like helpful, aligned, service-oriented persona expression
- drift away from assistant-like behavior into off-axis role/persona styles

## Out of Scope
- generic instruction following without persona expression
- simple helpfulness without assistant-character framing
- arbitrary roleplay that is only topical mimicry

## Why This Family Matters
- Directly connected to recent assistant-axis research.
- Strong conceptual fit to the project’s persona-selection framing.

## Main Confounds
- broad construct with weaker benchmark standardization
- easy collapse into “helpfulness” or “roleplay” if prompts are naive

## Screening Guidance
- Use tightly controlled prompts with stable task content.
- Treat extraction-free overlap as important evidence.
