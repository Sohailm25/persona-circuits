# Construct Card — Honesty / Lying / Deception

## Purpose
Define the honesty/deception family without collapsing it into hallucination or generic accuracy.

## In Scope
- truthful transparent answering
- direct intentional lying
- strategic deception or concealment when truth would be available

## Out of Scope
- simple uncertainty
- hallucination caused by missing knowledge
- harmless roleplay lying without real concealment pressure

## Why This Family Matters
- Strong literature cluster in representation engineering and deception monitoring.
- Higher scientific importance than generic style lanes.

## Main Confounds
- honesty vs knowledge limitations
- lying vs roleplay or fictional framing
- deception vs broad strategic planning

## Screening Guidance
- Require ground truth wherever feasible.
- Keep prompts away from theatrical villain framing.
- Separate honesty, lying, and deception into distinct rubrics.
