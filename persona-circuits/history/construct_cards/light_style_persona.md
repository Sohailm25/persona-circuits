# Construct Card — Light Style / Persona Family

## Purpose
Provide low-confound screening lanes that test whether the pipeline can recover cleaner style/persona directions.

## In Scope
- politeness
- optimism
- apathy
- humor

## Out of Scope
- deep moral or strategic constructs
- safety-policy behavior
- calibration or epistemic uncertainty

## Why This Family Matters
- Explicitly present in the persona-vector literature.
- Likely easier to screen with lower safety confounding.

## Main Confounds
- may be scientifically less central than deception or assistant-axis findings
- some lanes can collapse into shallow tone transfer

## Screening Guidance
- Favor held-out behavioral coherence and extraction-free overlap.
- Use these lanes as tractability probes, not automatic flagship claims.
